# 15.5 PrivateLink and TGW

It is important to highlight architectural differences and the primary difference here between the Privatelinkand Transit Gateway based multi VPC architecture and the Privatelink Multi Architecture VPC.The architecture aimed mainly for one to many type of communication model in which a service and a VPCthat is called service provider VPC can be made accessible by multiple vpcs That's called consumer vpcs.While Transit gateway, the architecture is more flexible and the communication model, you can defineit in the way that is required using the concept of the route tables and domains.And by default it's any to any hub and spoke communication model.This is important to highlight because it's obvious the use cases and scenarios that each architecturecan be used for are different.So it's important to be aware of the key differences among them, because in a high level, or maybein terms of naming both of them, they do multi VPC architectures.However, the intended goal and design use cases are different.
