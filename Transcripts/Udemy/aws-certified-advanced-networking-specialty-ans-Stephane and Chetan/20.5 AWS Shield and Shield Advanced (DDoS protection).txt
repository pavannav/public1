if your users are using your application, your website, then the attacker willjust send too many requests to your application server so that your application is overwhelmed withrespect to the CPU, memory and the network, and your legitimate users cannot access your application.So that is called denial of service attack.And very common denial of service attack is called DDoS attack that is internet server will send you back the response.Even though you haven't initiated those requests.So that's called UDP reflection attack.Then there is DNS flood attack, which targets your DNS server.And you know that DNS are like phone books.So if you don't have DNS resolution for your DNS query, you don't get the IP address of the applicationserver and no one can reach to your server.So it is very important that your DNS can handle all these attacks.And finally, there is a slow loris attack.And this is a very common attack and very difficult to detect.This is because all the traffic that comes to your server looks very legitimate.So for example, if you provide the login pages, the client will ask for the login page but will justnot respond with the next step.So they are very, very slow and they will try to be as slow as possible.So your server thinks that it is a legitimate traffic, but then has to keep all those connections open.So that's a slow loris attack.And as you
